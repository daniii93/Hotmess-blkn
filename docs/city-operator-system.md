# HotMess City Operator System

Every city can be operated by a local structure:

- City Lead.
- Community Lead.
- Partner Lead.
- Ambassador Team.

Roles:

- city_operator.
- city_manager.
- city_ambassador_lead.
- community_lead.
- partner_manager.

City operator may manage local events, hotels, community events, partners and ambassadors.

City operator may not change global platform settings, global membership, global roles or global revenue reports.
