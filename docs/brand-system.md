# HotMess Brand System

Positioning:

HotMess is a Luxury Community Experience Platform for events, travel and membership.

HotMess is not a club, party organizer or ticket shop. It is a premium community layer that connects curated nights, private weekends, hotels, partner benefits, membership and concierge.

Mission:

Create premium social experiences where people feel selected, respected and guided.

Vision:

Build a European network of curated communities, city weekends and membership experiences.

Brand promise:

Your HotMess weekend starts before the door opens.

Values:

- Belonging.
- Curated experiences.
- Respect.
- Confidence.
- Exploration.
- Quality.
- Privacy.
