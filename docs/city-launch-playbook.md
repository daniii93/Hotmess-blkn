# City Launch Playbook

Phase 1: Market analysis.

Phase 2: Community building.

Phase 3: Partner acquisition.

Phase 4: Launch event.

Phase 5: Membership build-up.

Phase 6: Scaling.

Launch checklist:

- Ambassador found.
- Partners won.
- Hotel partner active.
- Event location active.
- Membership benefits defined.
- City Guide created.
- Community event planned.
- Sponsors contacted.
